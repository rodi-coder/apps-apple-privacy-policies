Privacy Policy

Dolphin Dash

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Dolphin Dash ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile game developed for iOS devices. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our mobile game and is designed to be transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

Dolphin Dash is designed to be played offline and respects your privacy. We do not:

  • Create user accounts or require registration
  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Track your location or device location
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display third-party advertisements
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Local Storage

The app stores game progress locally on your device, including:

  • High scores and game statistics
  • Unlocked items and achievements
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Virtual currency balance (Credits)

Important: This data is stored only on your device and never leaves it. We have no access to this information and cannot retrieve it.

Apple Services Integration

The app integrates with optional Apple services. When you use these services, Apple handles your data according to their privacy policies:

  • Game Center: If you choose to sign in to Game Center, Apple manages your Game Center profile, leaderboard entries, and achievements. We only receive your Game Center display name and player ID for leaderboard functionality.
  • In-App Purchases: All purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, process, or store any payment information, credit card details, or billing addresses.
  • iCloud: If you have iCloud enabled, some game data may be backed up as part of your device backup. This is managed entirely by Apple.

For information about how Apple handles your data, please see: Apple Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Dolphin Dash is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users
  • The game contains no features that would allow users to share personal information
  • In-app purchases require parental authentication through the App Store
  • There is no user-generated content or social features

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of data breaches affecting your personal information through our app. All game data remains securely on your device.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and similar privacy regulations, you have certain rights regarding your personal data.

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to:

  • Access or provide to you
  • Correct or update
  • Delete or erase
  • Export or port

If you wish to delete your local game data, you can uninstall the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
