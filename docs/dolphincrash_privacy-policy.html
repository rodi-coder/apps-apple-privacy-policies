Privacy Policy

Dolphin Crash Game

Last updated: December 2025

1. General Information

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Dolphin Crash Game ("the App") handles user data.

Developer: Rodrigo do Carmo
Contact: dolphincrash@mailbox.org

2. Data Collection

The App itself does not actively collect or store personal data.

  • No registration is required
  • No custom user accounts are created
  • No names, email addresses, contact details, or location data are collected

3. Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use:

  • Third-party analytics tools
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Tracking technologies
  • Profiling or behavioral analysis mechanisms

4. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences, including children.

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. The App does not contain advertising, tracking, or monetization features.

5. Data Storage

Game progress and virtual credits are stored:

  • Locally on the user's device, and/or
  • Via Apple-provided services (e.g., iCloud), if enabled by the user

Important: This data is stored locally on the device and, if enabled by the user, may be included in Apple-managed device backups. The Developer does not have access to this data and cannot retrieve or view it.

6. Apple Services Integration

The App integrates with optional Apple services. When these services are used, Apple processes data in accordance with its own privacy policies.

Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Game Center, Apple manages your Game Center profile, leaderboard entries, and achievements. For leaderboard functionality, the App receives your Game Center display name and player identifier.

iCloud

If iCloud is enabled on your device, some game data may be backed up as part of Apple-managed device backups.

For information on how Apple handles data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of personal data
  • Restrict or object to data processing

Since the App does not actively collect or store personal data, these rights generally do not apply in practice. Locally stored game data can be removed at any time by uninstalling the App from your device.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the App's functionality changes. The current version will always be available within the App or its store listing.
